#Effect of Macro-economic Indicators and Financial Information on companies’ SP in Vietnam stock market: Panel Data Analysis from 2012-2022
Authors: Hoang Ngoc Anh Bui, Anh Khoi Nguyen, Pham Bao Nhi Luc, Le Anh Quan Dao, Pham Van Anh Ngo
Abstract: The research aims to investigate the impact of macroeconomics variables, financial data and technical factors on the SP of companies in the Vietnam market by randomly sampling on 19 companies on the Vietnam VN30-Index from 2012 to 2022. A panel regression model is used which examined both financial (banks and insurance institutions) and non-financial companies in the time length of 11 years in the Vietnam Stock Market. The data source of this research is primary and secondary data, which are macro-economic data and financial information for the period from the World Bank, companies' financial statements and Bank's Report. The results provide strong evidence that INF, GDP, NR and Dividend Paid Activity is significantly correlated with companies' SPs in the Vietnam Market. Although this paper presents divergent findings compared to other studies, it is significant that the observed correlations between macro variables, financial information, and dummy variables remain relevant to SP.
